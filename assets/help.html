Pixabay Images for Microsoft Word & PowerPoint – About, Licensing & Support information, Privacy Policy

About

Using this add-in you can search free (Creative Commons CC0 licensed) images on Pixabay right inside Word or PowerPoint – and insert them with one click.

Privacy

The add-in does not collect user information and does not transmit data to any party other than Pixabay, which is obviously necessary for the add-in to function.

Please review Pixabay's Privacy Policy for more information.

The add-in stores search queries and their results on the user's computer for caching purposes. This data is deleted after 24 hours, and it is never transmitted to another computer.
